Noodles

Noodles are a type of food made from unleavened dough which is rolled flat and cut, stretched or extruded, into long strips or strings. It is an easy to prepare meal that satisfies hunger. It is readily available in shops and comes in small packs

Ingredients Measurements
Noodles Two packs
Pepper Two tablespoons
Seasoning One tablespoon
  1. Put water on fire
  2. Allow to boil
  3. Add the noodles
  4. Add the seasoning
  5. Add the pepper
  6. Allow to boil for five minutes
  7. Serve while hot
